# Shoregate Consulting Landing Page

This is a modern, responsive landing page for Shoregate Consulting, designed to showcase AI-powered solutions for small and medium businesses (SMBs). The site includes a dedicated blog page (`blog-executive-metabolic-health.html`) covering executive metabolic health after 45, with integration to **Today's Food Coach**, an AI-powered nutrition tool.

## Features
- AI Readiness Assessments
- Quick-Win AI Pilots
- AI Strategy Workshops
- Blog: Executive Metabolic Health (after 45)
## Blog

**blog-executive-metabolic-health.html** — A comprehensive blog post on metabolic health for executives over 45. Covers:

- The executive metabolic crisis (insulin sensitivity, cortisol, weight gain)
- ROI of stable blood sugar (energy, cognition, weight management)
- 3-Levers Framework (Protein-First Eating, 10-Minute Walking Reset, Strategic Carb Timing)
- Why "eating less and exercising more" fails after 45
- 7-day executive metabolic reset plan
- Integration with **Today's Food Coach** AI-powered nutrition tool

**Assets referenced:**
- `businessman-eating-salad-desk.avif` — Featured image (executive with healthy desk meal)
- Links to Today's Food Coach guides:
  - Best Daily Eating Plan for Men Over 50
  - How to Lose Weight After 45 (Male)
  - Healthy Eating After 45
- Main CTA links to [todaysfoodcoach.com](https://todaysfoodcoach.com)

## How to Use
1. **Open `index.html`** in your browser to view the landing page.
2. **Customize Content:** Edit text, images, and links in `index.html` as needed for your business.
3. **Update Images:** Replace image files in the project directory to match your branding.
4. **Contact Form:** Submissions are sent via Formspree. Update the form action URL if you want to use a different service.
This site uses Google Analytics for traffic analysis. The tracking code is included in the `<head>` section of `index.html`. To use your own Google Analytics property, replace the tracking ID (`G-DE88TRRM15`) in the script tag with your own.

- `index.html` — Main landing page

## Customization
- **Colors & Styles:** Adjust CSS in the `<style>` block of `index.html` for branding.
- **Navigation:** Add or remove sections as needed.
- **Integrations:** Replace Formspree and Calendly links with your own services if desired.
- **Sitemap:** Update `sitemap.xml` when adding or removing pages. Change `<lastmod>` dates to reflect content updates for SEO.

## Search Engine Optimization
- The site includes a `sitemap.xml` file listing all pages.
- Submit the sitemap to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Update `<lastmod>` dates in `sitemap.xml` whenever content is modified.
- Ensure Google Analytics tracking ID (`G-DE88TRRM15`) is replaced with your own in production.
